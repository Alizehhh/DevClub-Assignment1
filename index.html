Some Common Myths that are far from Reality:

  1. Twice vaccination does not guarantee you're covid negative.
  2. Drinking Alcohol does not prevent COVID-19.
  3. Covid can be transmitted in cold as well as hot and humid climate.
  4. Thermometers cannot detect COVID-19.
  5. Spraying Chlorine or Alcohol on your body cannot kill the virus.
  6. Eating garlic does not prevent Covid-19.
  7. Rinsing your nose with saline solution cannot kill Corona virus.

Some Tips To Prevent COVID
